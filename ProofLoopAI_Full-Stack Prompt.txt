CORE INSIGHT

Every company already has proof that their product works.

The problem is that the proof is buried.

It exists inside:

* Customer emails
* Testimonials
* Reviews
* Support tickets
* Survey responses
* Sales call transcripts
* Community comments
* Customer success notes

Most companies never use this proof.

As a result:

* Marketing becomes generic
* Sales teams repeat the same work
* Founders struggle to create content
* Valuable trust signals are lost
* Customer success stories disappear

ProofLoop discovers hidden customer proof and transforms it into growth assets.

PROBLEM

Startups generate valuable customer proof every day, but it is scattered across conversations, documents, reviews, emails, and customer interactions.

Most of this proof is never reused.

Companies already possess evidence that their product works.

They simply fail to find it, rank it, and amplify it.

This leads to:

* Lower trust
* Lower conversion rates
* Longer sales cycles
* Weak positioning
* Generic marketing

* Outcomes
* ROI statements
* Trust signals
* Differentiators
* Objections overcome
* Emotional moments
* Switching reasons

The platform then generates:

1. Proof Scores
2. Trust Signal Rankings

4. Case Studies

7. LinkedIn Content
